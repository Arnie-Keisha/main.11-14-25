1nside T4ms: Our Vision

“1nside my T4ms” embodies the unity and identity of our group as students of FEU Roosevelt. The number “1” in 1nside symbolizes being one team that erflects our collaboration, solidarity, and shared goals throughout our first year. The number “4” in T4ms represents the four members of the group that contributes unique strengths and perspectives toward our collective growth. The term “Tams” stands for Tamaraws, signifying our green and glod pride and belonging as part of the FEU community. Altogether, “1nside my T4ms” captures the essence of our bond as one team of Tamaraws. This website serves as a documentation of our meaningful moments, experiences, and achievements during our first year in FEU Roosevelt.

Out logo represents the fusion of technology and teamwork. Its design elements reflect the innovative and connected nature of BSIT students, emphasizing our creativity and logical thinking as future IT professionals. The integration of the numbers “1” and “4” within the logo highligths both our individualtiy and our collaboration as one team. Altogether, “1nside my T4ms” and its logo illustrate our identity as one team of Tamaraws growing together through shared experiences and technological learning.
